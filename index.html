About SSP dataset

Uojdata is a University of juba website where data about SSP is found.
It is purposefully developed to easy data collection about a given topic.
This was made by university of juba students during their preparation for AI day, 2025.
The students who did it, made it so, such that those who shall be carrying out research will easily get it.
As technology advances the one who prioritized this task to have it ready on the table is highly appreciated and thanks prof. Dr.Felix for guidance he has provided.
Thanks to the student who made data colection and brought this website to life.
